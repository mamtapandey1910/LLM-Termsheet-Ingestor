TERM SHEET
Series A Preferred Stock Financing of Acme Technologies, Inc.

Date: February 15, 2026

This Term Sheet summarises the principal terms of a proposed Series A
preferred stock financing of Acme Technologies, Inc. (the "Company").
This document is non-binding except where expressly stated otherwise.

------------------------------------------------------------
FINANCING TERMS
------------------------------------------------------------

Issuer:              Acme Technologies, Inc.

Security:            Series A Preferred Stock ("Series A")

Aggregate Proceeds:  $5,000,000

Pre-Money Valuation: $20,000,000

Post-Money Valuation: $25,000,000

Price per Share:     $2.50 (the "Original Issue Price")

Investors:
  - Sequoia Capital ($3,000,000)
  - Andreessen Horowitz ($1,500,000)
  - Angel investors ($500,000)

Lead Investor:       Sequoia Capital

------------------------------------------------------------
TERMS OF SERIES A PREFERRED
------------------------------------------------------------

Liquidation Preference:
  1x non-participating liquidation preference equal to the Original
  Issue Price, plus any declared but unpaid dividends.

Anti-Dilution:
  Weighted-average anti-dilution protection, subject to standard
  carve-outs (stock splits, dividends paid in stock, etc.).

Dividends:
  8% per annum, cumulative, payable when and if declared by the Board.

Conversion:
  Automatically converts to Common Stock upon a qualified IPO of at
  least $50,000,000 at a pre-money valuation of at least $150,000,000.

Voting Rights:
  Series A votes on an as-converted basis together with Common Stock,
  except as required by law or the Certificate of Incorporation.

------------------------------------------------------------
BOARD OF DIRECTORS
------------------------------------------------------------

Board Composition:
  - 2 seats: Common Stock holders (founders)
  - 1 seat: Series A holder (Sequoia Capital designee)
  - 1 seat: Independent director (mutually agreed)

------------------------------------------------------------
OTHER TERMS
------------------------------------------------------------

Pro-Rata Rights:
  Each Investor shall have the right to participate in subsequent
  financing rounds on a pro-rata basis.

Information Rights:
  Investors holding at least 5% of Series A shall be entitled to
  receive monthly financial statements and an annual budget.

Governing Law:     State of Delaware

Closing Date:      On or before March 31, 2026

Counsel:
  Company:   Wilson Sonsini Goodrich & Rosati
  Investors: Fenwick & West LLP

------------------------------------------------------------
This Term Sheet is intended solely as a basis for further discussion
and is not intended to be binding.
